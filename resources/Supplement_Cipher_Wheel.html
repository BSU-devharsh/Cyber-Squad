Build Your Own Cipher Wheel

Cyber Squad 2026 - Day 3. Print this page, cut out both disks, and pin them at the center.

Outer disk (fixed - plaintext)
Inner disk (rotates - ciphertext)
  1. Print this page. Cut out the large outer disk and the smaller inner disk.
  2. Place the small disk on top of the large one, centers aligned. Push a split pin (brad) through both centers so the inner disk can spin.
  3. To encrypt with a shift of 3, turn the inner disk so its A points at the outer D. Now read inner-to-outer.
  4. To decrypt, line up the same shift and read outer-to-inner.
  5. Pick a secret shift, encode a message, and challenge a teammate to crack it without the shift.
