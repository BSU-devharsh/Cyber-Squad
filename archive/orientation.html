What is a cyber range?

Your safe practice arena

A cyber range is an isolated, cloud-based practice lab. You can run real security tools without touching the public internet or any real victim.

  • Everything stays inside the range.
  • You learn offense and defense legally and safely.

The one rule that matters most

Ethics first

You are training to be a white hat: an ethical hacker who always has permission.

Only test the training targets your instructor provides. Never real systems, never without permission.
