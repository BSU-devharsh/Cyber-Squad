# Safety and Ethics Agreement

Every Cyber Squad member agrees to these rules before touching any tool.

1. **Permission only.** I will only test, scan, or attack systems inside this cyber range, on the training targets my instructor provides. I will never target real websites, accounts, or devices.
2. **Stay in the range.** All activity stays inside the isolated lab. Nothing I do should reach the public internet.
3. **Protect people.** I will never harass, dox, impersonate, or threaten anyone. OSINT practice uses only public information and only approved practice targets.
4. **No real secrets.** I will never enter a real password or personal data into any practice tool. I use made-up examples.
5. **Report, do not exploit.** If I find a real security problem outside of class, I will tell a trusted adult and not exploit it.
6. **Be a white hat.** I am learning these skills to protect people and systems, not to harm them.

White hat = an ethical hacker who always has permission. OSINT = Open-Source Intelligence.
